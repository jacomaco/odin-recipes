Ragu recipe

Description

This is a recipe for the beloved italian classic Ragu

Ingredients

  1. 1 Large yellow onion
  2. 3 Sticks of celeri
  3. 3 Carrots
  4. Salt
  5. Pepper
  6. 800 Grams of ground beef
  7. 1 Dl of red dry wine
  8. 1 dl of tomato puree
  9. 800 Grams of passed tomatoes
  10. 1 Litre of water
  11. 3 Bayleafs
